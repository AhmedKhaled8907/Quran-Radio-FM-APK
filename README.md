### **Quran App**

A feature-rich Quran streaming app built with Flutter, designed with **Clean Architecture** and **BLoC** to ensure scalability, maintainability, and testability.  
The app provides a smooth Quran listening experience through **reciters & surahs**, **live radio stations**, and a dedicated **audio player**, along with personalization features like **favorites**, **search**, **localization**, and **theme switching**.

## Brief explanation

This app focuses on providing a complete Quran audio experience:

- **Reciters & Surahs:** Browse reciters, select a moshaf, and explore the full list of surahs.
- **Audio Player:** Play surahs with smooth playback controls and queue support.
- **Live Radios:** Stream Quran radio stations with quick access and stable playback.
- **Favorites:** Save radios, reciters, and surahs for fast access later.
- **User Experience:** Search across content, switch between **Dark/Light** themes, and use the app in **Arabic/English**.
